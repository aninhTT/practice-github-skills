# Sam Customer Email Helper

## What It Does

Creates a fictional customer-support email response using a made-up scenario and a friendly, clear tone.

## When To Use It

Use this skill when practicing how to structure a response workflow without using real customer messages.

## Inputs

- A fictional customer question.
- The desired tone.
- A made-up policy or answer.
- Any constraints for the response.

## Output

A polished fictional email draft and a short explanation of the tone choices.

## Example Prompt

```text
Draft a friendly email to a fictional customer asking whether a community pottery class allows beginners.
```

## Safety Notes

Do not include real customer messages, names, account details, support tickets, company policies, or private data.
