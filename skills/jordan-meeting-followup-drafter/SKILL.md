# Jordan Meeting Followup Drafter

## What It Does

Drafts a fictional follow-up note after a pretend project meeting, including decisions, open questions, and next steps.

## When To Use It

Use this skill when practicing how to summarize a meeting-like scenario without relying on real meeting notes or private context.

## Inputs

- A fictional meeting topic.
- A short list of made-up attendees.
- Fake decisions.
- Fake action items.

## Output

A concise follow-up note with a summary, decisions, and action items.

## Example Prompt

```text
Write a follow-up note for a fictional meeting about planning a neighborhood book swap.
```

## Safety Notes

Do not use real transcripts, private meeting notes, company strategy, customer details, or personal commitments.
